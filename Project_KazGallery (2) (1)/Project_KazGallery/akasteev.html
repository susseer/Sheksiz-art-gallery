Abilkhan Kasteev – The Father of Kazakh National Painting

Abilkhan Kasteev (1904–1973) is celebrated as the pioneer of Kazakh national painting. Born in the village of Chizhin, Kazakhstan, Kasteev’s work reflects the cultural heritage and history of Kazakhstan, while also embracing the transformations of the 20th century.

Kasteev studied at the N. Gogol Art Studio in Almaty, and his works often depicted the beauty of the Kazakh landscape, the people’s daily lives, and significant historical events. His art merges traditional Kazakh themes with European painting techniques, making his contributions truly unique.
