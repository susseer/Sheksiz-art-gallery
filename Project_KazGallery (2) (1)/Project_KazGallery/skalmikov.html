Sergei Kalmikov – The Eccentric Visionary

Sergei Kalmikov (1891–1967) was a unique and eccentric painter, poet, and visionary who spent much of his life in Almaty, Kazakhstan. His work is characterized by its surreal, mystical, and often futuristic themes. Although little-known during his lifetime, Kalmikov created hundreds of works that reflect his inner world of dreams and visions, often blending fantasy with reality.

Kalmikov’s work was deeply influenced by Symbolism, and he often painted in vibrant, unearthly colors, creating otherworldly landscapes and scenes. His art stood out for its bold experimentation and unusual approach to perspective and color.
